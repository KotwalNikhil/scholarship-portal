Steps To Apply Scholarship

  1. First step is to complete your profile with every detail correct because that detail will be used in your scholarship application form
  2. If there are any scholarship active you will be able to see then on your dashboard else you will see no active scholarship
  3. navigate to the scholarship you want to apply for, read the instructions and download the scholarship application form from their and fill the application form
  4. after this navigate to apply button
  5. you will see the online application form there,verify your details weather they are correct or not
  6. you will see your documents already fetched from your profile, if you wish to change the documents you can just change them in your profile
  7. if their are any others documents required for that particular scholarship upload them in the other documents 1 and other document 2 space
  8. also upload the copy of scholarship application form that you downloaded and filled
  9. after that click on submit and that's it

Keep in mind

  • all the documents other than scholarship application form should in jpg,jpeg or png format
  • scholarship application should be in pdf format
  • size of documents should be strictly less than 1mb
  • If in your profile, portal is showing the path of current document but the document is not visible below then try to clear the previous document by clicking on the clear checkbox and then update profile.
